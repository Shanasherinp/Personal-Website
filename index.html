About

Hi, I’m Shana Sherin, a passionate Web Scraping Consultant with a background in Commerce and a story of bold transformation. While I began my academic journey with a B.Com degree, my genuine curiosity for technology and problem-solving pushed me to explore the world of programming — and I haven’t looked back since.

With no formal IT degree, I committed myself to learning Python, Django, REST APIs, and eventually discovered my niche in web scraping using the Scrapy framework. What started as self-study evolved into real-world expertise, and today, I proudly work as a consultant with a Texas-based MNC, helping teams extract valuable data efficiently and at scale.

From e-commerce and real estate data to lead generation and business intelligence, I’ve worked on building clean, optimized, and scalable scraping pipelines. Every project fuels my love for automation, clean code, and continuous learning.

I'm now focused on growing deeper into backend development and automation — and one day, I aim to launch my own tech venture. For now, I’m enjoying the ride and evolving every single day, one script at a time.
